Geores

Branding Identity • 2011

Above
The final dynamic identity created for geores.
company

Geores is an Italian company that deals with concrete activities on geo-tech, hydrogeology and geophysics - some of their clients are Eni, Enel and Johnson&Johnson.

Branding

I had the pleasure to work on this branding while I was working at the agency Cappelli Identity Design with the role of Art Director. The main purpose of this project was focussed on brand and corporate image, to show the stratification of different elements in a smart way. I decided to create a visual system that generates a sphere through the gradual overlapping of the elements.

The movement that generates this shape, a rotation on a 23° axis, references the angle of descent of the earth’s axis and strengthens the link between the brand and the Earth.
