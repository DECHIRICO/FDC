The Iconic

Above
The final logo created for The Iconic.

The Iconic launched in 2011 under e-commerce incubator Rocket Internet GmBh. It's the Australia’s largest online fashion retailer and is one of Australia’s largest online fashion retailers, creating a growing home-based fashion community and quickly became a household name with over 90% brand recognition in under 3 years.

brief

A new brand

The branding objective was to create an icon, representative of both premium, mainstream fashion and sport, that supports the future growth of variousdepartments under The Iconic umbrella.When creating the visual identity from a technical perspective, I built a foundation that would enable it to be recognised across all platforms.
