theright.fit

Theright.fit is a start up lunched by Taryn Williams and Aurelien Labonne that aims to revolutionise the modelling and talent industry by removing hidden commissions and bias, empowering users of the platform with the information and facts they need to make informed decisions about their next job or casting.

I designed the branding and the identity - creating a recognizable visual language for online and offline applications.
